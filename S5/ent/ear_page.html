2. Which of the following nerve pass through middle ear

  • A. vagus nerve
  • B. glossopharyngeal nerve
  • C. vestibulocochlear nerve
  • D. hypoglossal nerve
  • E. facial nerve

Correct answer: E

d. Incorrect. The hypoglossal nerve controls tongue movements.
e. Correct. The facial nerve, especially its chorda tympani branch, traverses the middle ear cavity.
